Meet The Dream Team

Elena Seaholm

Trained as a bioengineer, she combines her technical training with her love for art. Most interested in incorporating light, sculpture, and symbolism, her favorite projects are wacky, intricate photography shoots and light-focused ceramics.

Emily Seaholm

After a career in Nursing and years of heavy involvement in school art programs, Emily got dragged into a business model for her art by Elena. She is the model, the inspiration, our guiding light! Her favorite project is, without a doubt, the scarecrows.
